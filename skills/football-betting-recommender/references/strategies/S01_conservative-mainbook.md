## Entry Signals

Enter only when at least two independent signals align:

- Tactical baseline supports the selection.
- Current lineup/news does not invalidate the baseline.
- Market line or odds still has value.
- Public heat appears to overprice the favorite.
- Total-goal expectation matches pace, matchup, and motivation.

## Staking

- Default single-bet stake: 3%-5% of bankroll.
- High-confidence mainbook stake: 6%-8%.
- Same-match exposure cap: 8%-10%.
- 串关 under this strategy: 1%-2%, used only to combine conservative legs.

## Forbidden Conditions

- Do not chase a favorite simply because it is famous.
- Do not add live exposure in the same direction after the match state clearly disproves the pre-match thesis.
- Do not bet when lineups are uncertain and the edge depends on one or two key players.
- Do not force a bet when Asian handicap and totals point to conflicting match scripts.
