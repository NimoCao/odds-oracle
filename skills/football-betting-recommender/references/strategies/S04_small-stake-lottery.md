# S04 Small Stake Lottery

## Positioning

Use this strategy for high-odds speculative plays: 波胆, exact score, small parlays, and other small-stake upside bets. It must stay separate from main-bankroll logic.

## Suitable Markets

- 波胆/exact score.
- Small 2-leg or 3-leg parlays.
- Narrow score bands such as 0-0, 1-0, 1-1, 2-0, 2-1 when the match script is unusually clear.

## Entry Signals

Use only when a score band has a defensible match script:

- Expected total goals are concentrated in a narrow range.
- One side's attack route and the other side's defensive weakness point to a specific score shape.
- Motivation supports either conservative control or one-sided pressure.
- The price is high enough to justify low hit rate.

## Staking

- Default stake: 0.5%-1.5% of bankroll.
- Hard cap per ticket: 2%.
- Hard cap per match for lottery bets: 3%.
- Never increase lottery stake after misses.

## Forbidden Conditions

- Do not use this as the main recommendation.
- Do not scatter too many exact scores in the same match.
- Do not combine multiple fragile assumptions just to create a large payout.
- Do not mix lottery bets into the mainbook exposure cap as if they are hedge or value bets.

## Output Bias

Give a small score pool and state that it is optional. Main recommendation should remain separate if one exists.
