# S03 Live Correction

## Positioning

Use this strategy for live market overreaction. It is a correction strategy, not a chase-loss strategy.

## Suitable Markets

- Live Asian handicap.
- Live totals.
- Next goal or draw-no-bet style positions when the platform supports them.
- Hedge or take-profit decisions after the market moves in favor.

## Entry Signals

Enter only when live data independently supports the action:

- Scoreline and chance quality disagree, creating market overreaction.
- Losing side has higher or rapidly improving xG, shots, box entries, and territory.
- Early goal came from penalty, own goal, low-xG shot, or isolated error while open-play control points the other way.
- Leading side is not controlling tempo despite the score.
- Current odds still compensate for time decay and volatility.

## Staking

- Default live stake: 1%-3%.
- Strong correction with clear xG edge: 4%-5%.
- Same-match live cap: 5%, unless hedging existing exposure.
- Reduce stake when data is delayed or xG is unavailable.

## Forbidden Conditions

- Do not add same-direction exposure just because a pre-match bet is losing.
- Do not bet without current minute, score, and at least shots/tempo data.
- Do not chase after a red card without reassessing shape and motivation.
- Do not play a line after the stated valid window has passed.
- Do not recommend live over when goals already exceed xG and tempo has collapsed.
